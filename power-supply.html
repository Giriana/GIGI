Power Supply Unit (PSU)

Converts power for all computer components

Description and Function

The PSU converts AC power from the wall to DC power needed by computer components and regulates voltage.

Key specifications:

  • Wattage: Total power output (500W-1200W typical)
  • Efficiency: 80 Plus ratings (Bronze to Titanium)
  • Modularity: Non-modular, semi-modular, or fully modular
  • Connectors: 24-pin, CPU, PCIe, SATA, etc.

Key functions:

  1. Convert AC to DC power
  2. Regulate voltage levels
  3. Provide overload protection
  4. Distribute power to components

Troubleshooting Tips

  • No power: Check connections and power switch
  • Random shutdowns: May indicate insufficient wattage
  • Burning smell: Immediately disconnect power
